Pasta Carbonara

A classic Italian dish made with spaghetti, eggs, cheese, and pancetta or guanciale.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While pasta is cooking, whisk together eggs, grated cheeses, and a pinch of black pepper in a bowl.
  3. In a skillet, cook diced pancetta or guanciale over medium heat until crispy.
  4. Drain cooked spaghetti, reserving some pasta water. Immediately add hot spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta, tossing to coat.
  5. Remove skillet from heat. Quickly pour in the egg and cheese mixture, tossing continuously to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water as needed to thin out the sauce.
  6. Serve immediately with additional grated cheese and black pepper.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Soft and chewy cookies loaded with chocolate chips, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (230g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (220g) packed light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups (360g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups (340g) semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350째F (175째C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  3. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually stir into the creamed mixture.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto prepared baking sheets.
  6. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in preheated oven, or until edges are golden brown. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Grilled BBQ Chicken

Juicy chicken marinated in BBQ sauce and grilled to perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup (240ml) BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together BBQ sauce, olive oil, honey, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Place chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag or shallow dish. Pour marinade over chicken, turning to coat. Seal or cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or up to 24 hours).
  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Oil the grill grate.
  4. Remove chicken from marinade, shaking off excess. Discard remaining marinade.
  5. Grill chicken for 6 to 7 minutes per side, or until juices run clear and internal temperature reaches 165째F (75째C).
  6. Remove from grill and let rest for a few minutes before serving.
